The default Vercel URL is long because it's auto-generated. You can make it short and professional in 2 minutes.

## Option 1: Shorten the Vercel Link (Free)
You can change `srm-cia-survey-xyz...vercel.app` to something like `srm-survey.vercel.app`.

1. Go to your **Vercel Dashboard**: [https://vercel.com/dashboard](https://vercel.com/dashboard)
2. Click on your project (**srm-cia-survey**).
3. Go to **Settings** (top tab).
4. Click **Domains** (left sidebar).
5. You will see the long URL there. Click **Edit**.
6. Change it to something short, e.g., `srm-cia-survey.vercel.app`.
   - *Note: If that name is taken, try `srm-cia-feedback.vercel.app` etc.*
7. Click **Save**.

## Option 2: Use Your Own Domain (Professional)
If you own a domain (like `srmcia.com`), you can use a subdomain like `survey.srmcia.com`.

1. In the same **Domains** section on Vercel.
2. Type your custom domain (e.g., `survey.yourcompany.com`) in the input box.
3. Click **Add**.
4. Vercel will show you some DNS records (CNAME/A records).
5. Log in to where you bought your domain (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) and add those records.
6. It will automatically secure it with HTTPS.

## Option 3: Bit.ly (Quickest)
If you just want a link to share on WhatsApp/Email right now:
1. Copy your long Vercel link.
2. Go to [bit.ly](https://bit.ly).
3. Paste it and get a short link like `bit.ly/srm-survey`.
